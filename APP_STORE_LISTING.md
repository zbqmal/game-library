Last updated: [DATE]

## Overview

Game Library ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to respect your privacy.

## Information We Collect

### Locally Stored Data

- Game scores are stored locally on your device using local storage
- Player names entered for leaderboards are stored locally
- No data is transmitted to external servers

### Analytics (if applicable)

We do not currently collect any analytics data. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated.

## Data Usage

- All game data stays on your device
- We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data
- We do not track your gameplay or behavior

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website.

- Email: support@yourdomain.com
- GitHub: https://github.com/zbqmal/game-library

---

This privacy policy is effective as of [DATE].

- [ ] All games tested thoroughly

- [ ] Proper version number set

---

## Tips for App Store Success

1. **Screenshots**: Show actual gameplay, not just the home screen
2. **Description**: Focus on benefits, not just features
3. **Keywords**: Research competitors and popular search terms
4. **Icon**: Make it distinctive and recognizable at small sizes
5. **Updates**: Plan to update regularly with bug fixes and new features
6. **Reviews**: Respond promptly to user reviews and feedback
7. **Ratings**: Encourage satisfied users to leave positive reviews
